About

Animal Crossing is a video game series made by Nintendo. It was exported for the west in 2001, with the first title in the series, “Animal Crossing.” The latest title in the series “Animal Crossing New Horizons” and is being played by millions of people worldwide. This websites purpose is to help those who play Animal Crossing with tips and tricks to improve their experiences as well as to find a community for the game.

Villager Of The Day

Daisy Mae

Today, May 5th is her birthday! She is a special visitor introduced in "Animal Crossing: New Horizons." She arrives at the player's island every Sunday morning (from 5 AM to 12 PM) to sell turnips, taking on the role from her grandmother Joan.

She is a small, orange boar with brown hair styled in straight bangs. Small curls peak out of her white head scarf. A notable feature is her nose drip, which hangs from her left nostril.
